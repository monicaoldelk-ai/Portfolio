Monica Old Elk-East

My name is Monica Old Elk-East. I am was born on the Crow Agency Reservation, Montana. My hometown while growing up was in Sierra Vista, Arizona. Upstate New York is my current home of domicile.

I live with my partner and our two tabby cats. We both have a boy named Nelson and a girl named Princess. We adopted them from the Saratoga Animal Shelter, New York in 2024. They have lived with us for over one year and bring us a lot of joy.




For fun, I enjoy writing, reading, learning, exercising, gaming, putting puzzles together, and hiking the Adirondack Mountains. I also enjoy spending time with my family and friends. Running and listening to music are other passions as it allows me to be outside among nature.

Previously, I was an over-the-road truck driver who had her CDL. It has allowed me to drive in 48 states in the United States and in Germany.

I've recently found myself in the coding world as I am currently taking a Front-End Web Development course through CanCode. CanCode is an organization offered through the state of New York.
